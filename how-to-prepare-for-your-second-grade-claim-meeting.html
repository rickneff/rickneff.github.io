How to Prepare for Your Second Grade Claim Meeting

Read and Heed

The most important advice I can give you is to pray to know what grade you should claim. You and your Heavenly Father know which of the grade descriptions spelled out in the syllabus best describes you.

To refresh your memory, please reread the syllabus where it talks about grading. Then

  1. Double check that your Course Tracker table is complete, with the grade you will claim decided beforehand and recorded (see number 5).
  2. Bringing your computer will not be required this time. Instead, you will make your work available via a github (or equivalent) repository.
  3. Create a private repository that you will have imported your work files to, and that you will have invited me to collaborate on.
  4. If you have never done anything like number 3 before, my TAs will show you how. Or make an appointment with me for hands-on help.
  5. Bring a filled-out and signed printed copy of the other tab on this page, named Readiness Certification.
  6. Be right on time. Punctuality is critical to the smooth flow of these meetings. Even though I have a buffer of 5 minutes between back-to-back scheduled meetings, if you are even two minutes late I may ask you to reschedule.
